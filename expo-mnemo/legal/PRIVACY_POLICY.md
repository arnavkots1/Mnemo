# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: December 29, 2025**

## Introduction

Mnemo ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

## Information We Collect

### Data You Provide
- **Photos**: Images you capture or import into the App
- **Audio Recordings**: Voice recordings you create within the App
- **Text Notes**: Written notes and descriptions you add to memories
- **Location Data**: Location information when you grant location permissions

### Data Collected Automatically
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system version
- **Usage Data**: How you interact with the App
- **Location Data**: If you enable passive location tracking, we collect location data in the background

## How We Use Your Information

### Primary Uses
- **Memory Creation**: To create and store your personal memories
- **AI Processing**: To generate summaries and analyze your memories using AI services (Google Gemini API)
- **App Functionality**: To provide core features like photo storage, audio playback, and location tagging

### AI Processing
- Your photos, audio, and notes may be sent to Google's Gemini AI service for analysis and summary generation
- This processing helps create meaningful daily summaries and memory descriptions
- Data sent to Gemini is processed according to Google's privacy policies

## Data Storage

### Local Storage
- **Primary Storage**: All your memories are stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage
- **No Cloud Backup**: We do not automatically sync or backup your data to cloud services
- **User Control**: You can delete all data at any time through the App settings

### Third-Party Services
- **Google Gemini API**: When generating AI summaries, data is temporarily sent to Google's servers
- **No Permanent Storage**: We do not store your data on third-party servers permanently
- **Processing Only**: Data is sent for processing and returned to your device

## Data Security

- **Local Encryption**: Data stored on your device uses device-level encryption
- **Secure Transmission**: Data sent to AI services uses HTTPS encryption
- **No Account Required**: We do not require account creation, reducing data exposure risk

## Your Rights

### Access and Control
- **View Your Data**: All your memories are accessible within the App
- **Delete Data**: You can delete individual memories or all data at any time
- **Export Data**: You can export your data through device backup features

### Permissions
- **Camera**: Required to capture photos for memories
- **Microphone**: Required to record audio for memories
- **Location**: Optional - used to tag memories with location data
- **Photo Library**: Required to import existing photos

## Children's Privacy

Mnemo is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Third-Party Services

### Google Gemini API
- We use Google's Gemini AI service for memory analysis
- Your data is processed according to Google's privacy policy
- Data is not stored permanently on Google's servers

## Data Retention

- **Local Storage**: Data remains on your device until you delete it
- **AI Processing**: Data sent to AI services is processed and not retained
- **Deletion**: When you delete data, it is permanently removed from your device
